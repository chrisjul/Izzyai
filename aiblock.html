Application of AI Blockchain

Artificial Intelligence are two very distinct technologies that are often perceive as never being able to be compatible, but the combination of artificial intelligence and blockchain technology creates the perfect combination for the management of large databases

Data Protection

The progress of AI is completely dependent on the input of data. Through data, AI receives information about the world and things happening on it. Basically, data feeds AI, and through it, AI will be able to continuously improve itself.

Blockchain is a technology that allows for the encrypted storage of data on a distributed ledger. It allows for the creation of fully secured databases which can be looked into by the individuals who approved the use of their data. When combining blockchains with AI, companies create a backup system for the sensitive and valuable personal data of individuals. This is very important for healthcare and financial companies.

Smart Computer Power

If you were to operate a blockchain, with all its encrypted data, on a computer you would need very strong hashing processing power. The algorithms used to mine Bitcoin blocks, consists in enumerating all the possible miners to provide a solution and checking whether each candidate satisfies the transaction problem before verifying it.

AI allows us to move away from this and tackle tasks in a more intelligent, practical, and efficient way. Imagine a machine learning-based algorithm, which could practically make the whole process seamless by just providing it the proper data and training.
